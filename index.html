Rachael Nike · Black Poppy Architect · Est. 1624

"My first memory is my mother's garden — roses and California poppies."

The Turtle Oracle · Kindred Soul Charts was born one week after I lost my 230-pound black brindle English Mastiff, Turtle — my MS service animal, my anchor, the steadiest soul I have ever known. He stayed for nearly 12 years. He outlived his breed by four of them. Through losing Trent, my Tervuren. Through losing my mother Misha. Through every dark night that needed holding.

In the raw middle of that grief, this work arrived — the need to understand not just who he was, but why he chose me. What the stars were doing the day he came. What every lean of that massive body against my legs actually meant.

Turtle gave me this work. Trent watches over it. Alice Moon carries it forward.

Every chart is cast from actual planetary data — positions, elements, moon phase at birth — and interpreted through an ancestral framework I created to honor the bond between animals and their humans. Every chart is as unique as the soul it's cast for. Delivered as a sacred PDF within 48 hours.
